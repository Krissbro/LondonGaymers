# Marry
You can now marry your loved one!

## Marry Commands
- `[p]marry <@yourlovedone>` marry your loved one!
- `[p]divorce <divorce_id>` divorce your ex.
- `[p]forcemarry <person> <lovedone>`¹ marry someone to his or her loved one.

## Annotations
¹ bot owner only.
² moderators only.

<> Needed

[] Optional

[p] means prefix.
